====== 2. Theramin of the Winds ======
Created Thursday 23 January 2020

Theramin (musical instrument) that can cause one of the following pairs of effects:

1. Spread a sense of nihilism/still the air
2. Spread a sense of optimistic wonder and energy/gusts of wind
3. Spread a sense of panic and discord/gale force winds
4. Spread a sense of calm and serenity/steady and gentle winds

Can also be set on the ground to function as an alarm spell
