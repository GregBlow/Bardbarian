====== 2. Expedition ======
Created Friday 20 September 2019

The band, such as it is, has been hired to play entertainment on a meandering passenger ship. 

An artificer by the name of Willett Timpson, who have paid the captain and owner of the ship a fee to anchor for a period of time off of an island in the Selachian ocean, in pursuit of an artifact.

A Paladin of the Caravansery, Jianna (Reede) is escorting a runic chest to the home temple in the city, with a guard of acolytes. Inside are packed ~1000 flash frozen Guizem's Lilies (~5,000,000gp).

Among the crew is a spy/saboteur for pirates. Among their other traits, they are a terrible poet. (Roll insight to determine if you think it's good)
