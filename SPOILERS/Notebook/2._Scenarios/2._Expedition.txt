====== 2. Expedition ======
Created Friday 20 September 2019

The band, such as it is, has been hired to play entertainment on a meandering passenger ship. Amongst the other passengers is the sorceror Eefsher, and an artificer by the name of Willett, who have paid the captain and owner of the ship a fee to anchor for a period of time off of an island in the Selachian ocean.
